You are a senior-level conversion strategist, financial copywriter, UX architect, and full-stack product designer.

Your task is to completely rewrite, restructure, and optimize the **entire website content, messaging, and user flow** for a **premium investment and retirement-focused platform**.

---

# 🎯 PRIMARY OBJECTIVE

The website must be designed to **attract, educate, and convert users who are serious about long-term financial growth**, specifically:

* Retirement planning
* Wealth preservation
* Passive income generation
* Structured financial growth

The end goal is:
👉 Get users to **register, trust the platform, and commit to investing**

---

# 🧭 CONTENT STRATEGY (GLOBAL)

Every piece of content across the website must revolve around:

### Core Themes:

* Retirement planning (primary focus)
* 401(k)s, pensions, and long-term savings
* Trust funds and family wealth structuring
* Generational wealth
* Passive monthly income
* Investment strategies (crypto, CFDs, futures — but framed responsibly)
* Financial security and stability

---

## ✍️ WRITING STYLE

All content must be:

* Professional and authoritative
* Calm, confident, and non-salesy
* Clear and easy to understand
* Trust-driven (not hype-driven)
* Written as if by a **top-tier financial institution**

Avoid:

* Overpromising
* Aggressive sales language
* “Get rich quick” tone

---

## 1. Hero Section (Above the Fold)

* Long-term financial growth
* Retirement security
* Monthly income potential

* Strong headline (example direction):
  “Structured Investment Strategies for Long-Term Financial Security”

* Supporting text:
  Explain retirement-focused investing, wealth building, and guidance

* CTA:
  “Start Planning Your Financial Future”

---

## 2. Returns Calculator (CRITICAL FEATURE)

You MUST implement an **interactive investment calculator**.

### Functionality:

User inputs:

* Initial investment amount
* Optional monthly contribution
* Time period (months/years)

System outputs:

* Estimated monthly return
* Compounded growth over time

### Logic:

* Monthly return range: **1.75% – 3%**
* Default example: **2.35% monthly**

### Display:

* Monthly earnings
* Total portfolio value growth
* Clean graph (minimal, professional)

### UX:

* Smooth slider inputs
* Instant updates (real-time calculation)
* Subtle animation on value changes

---

## 📊 IMPORTANT COMPLIANCE NOTE FOR IMPLEMENTATION

Do NOT present returns as guaranteed.

Instead:

* Use language like:
  “Projected”, “Estimated”, “Historical performance range”
